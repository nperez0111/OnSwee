  1. How to play:

  2. You click on any square to place a piece on the game board. For the first 6 rounds of this game you and your opponent are not allowed to make a line through the center blue block be it, diagnal, vertical or horizontal, then after those rounds pass the object of the game is to make a line using the center block diagnally, vertically or horizontally by dragging your pieces. You also are not allowed at anytime to drag a piece from one gray block to another, you may move into an unoccupied space that is within a block radius other than this exception.

    You and your opponent each have three pieces. For the first 3 rounds you will be placing your pieces wherever you like so long as it is not in a line through the center.The next 3 rounds you will drag (or swipe) your pieces to an adjacent spot that is empty while still not being able to create a line through the center blue block. Subsequently, you will proceed to play the rest of the game attempting to make a line through the center blue block before your opponent, whoever makes such a line Wins.

  3. Player1 will go first, simply click on a block of your choosing to start the game.
